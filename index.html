YOUTH SKATING
CONFERENCE
2022

The purpose of this conference is to engage the youth in this sport. For the youth to put their focus on the right things and not focus on the wrong things. We are going to be joined by the best Skating professionals in the world. COME ALL AND LET'S HAVE FUN.

2022-07-28(THU) ~ 2022-07-30(SAT)

@SKATECON, NAIROBI, KENYA

Program of the Day

  • Conference

  • The program starts with the Conference where all the speakers will get a chance to share their experiences in the Skating profession.

  • Exhibition

  • This is where the speakers and all the contestants will show off their skills and stand a chance to win a prize sponsored by Monster Energy Drink.

  • Meet & Greet

  • After the exhibition all attendants will meet and interact with the speakers. Get to know their favourite celebrity and their experiences.

  • Learn skate tricks from the speakers

  • This is the fun part. Together with all the Partners, we've teamed up with some of the greatest skating teachers, to teach the kids and youth to take their hobby to the next level.

  • Take Photos with Speakers

  • Finally, the kids will get to go back home with a smile, once they have taken pictures with the attending speakers. The aim is to put a smile on the kid.
